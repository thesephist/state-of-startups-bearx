State of Berkeley Startups

Cal's startup ecosystem is a microcosm of Silicon Valley. We at BearX took a deep dive into startups, founders, and events happening on BearX, Cal's entrepreneurship and innovation portal, to find out what problems Berkeley entrepreneurs are chasing next, and where we're headed as a community.

Software developers are the most wanted talent for Cal startups, with 16% of all job openings related to mobile or web development, machine learning, blockchain, or systems architecture.

But we're also seeing high demand for business development and analytics specialists, with almost a tenth of all job openings related to business.

BearX is a popular place for startups to recruit interns and find cofounders for equity, but 50% of all job openings on Bearx are paid positions, or positions with both pay and equity compensation.
